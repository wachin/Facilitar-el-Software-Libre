> ## Qt in use
> 
> In 2017, the Qt Company estimated a community of about 1 million developers worldwide[\[18\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-18) in over 70 industries.[\[19\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-19)
> 
> ### Desktop UIs
> 
> [![](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/KDE_Mascot_Konqi_for_Qt.png/250px-KDE_Mascot_Konqi_for_Qt.png)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:KDE_Mascot_Konqi_for_Qt.png)

> KDE's mascot [Konqi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Konqi "Konqi")
> 
> Graphical user-interfaces and [desktop environments](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desktop_environment "Desktop environment") that utilize Qt/QML as widget toolkit:
> 
> -   [KDE Plasma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KDE_Plasma "KDE Plasma"), a [libre](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libre_software "Libre software") [desktop environment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desktop_environment "Desktop environment") for various computing devices[\[20\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-20)
> -   DDE ([Deepin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deepin "Deepin") Desktop Environment) of Linux Deepin[\[21\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-21)[\[22\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-22)
> -   UKUI ([Ubuntu Kylin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Kylin "Ubuntu Kylin") User Interface)[\[23\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-23)
> -   CutefishDE, a desktop environment built with Qt Quick and essential KDE frameworks [\[24\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-24)
> -   [LXQt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LXQt "LXQt") (Lightweight X11 Desktop Environment)
> -   [Lumina](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lumina_\(desktop_environment\) "Lumina (desktop environment)"), a desktop environment designed for [BSD](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BSD "BSD")\-based TrueOS[\[25\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-25)
> -   [Lomiri](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lomiri "Lomiri") (formerly [Unity8](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unity8 "Unity8")),[\[26\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-26) a convergent desktop environment started by Canonical, maintained by Ubports[\[27\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-27)
> -   Maui Shell, a DE that also features their own app suite[\[28\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-28)
> -   [Unity 2D](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unity_\(user_interface\)#Unity_2D "Unity (user interface)"), a desktop shell written in Qt and Qml
> -   [Trinity DE](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trinity_Desktop_Environment "Trinity Desktop Environment"), a continuously developed fork based on KDE3[\[29\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_\(software\)#cite_note-29)
> -   [SDDM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simple_Desktop_Display_Manager "Simple Desktop Display Manager"), a [display manager](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/X_display_manager "X display manager") written in [QML](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/QML "QML") that is compatible with [X11](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/X11 "X11") and [Wayland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wayland_\(protocol\) "Wayland (protocol)").
